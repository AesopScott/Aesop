Jump to a Founder

    Most advisors bring deep expertise in one domain — brand, education, or technology. Founders bring all three. They are practitioners who have crossed disciplines, seen AI's promise from multiple vantage points, and arrived at the same conclusion: that getting AI education right for the next generation is not just important — it is urgent.

    These are not passive supporters. Founders Board members are active participants in every dimension of AESOP's work — shaping how we communicate, what we teach, and whether the platform is technically sound and trustworthy. Finding individuals willing to carry that breadth of commitment is rare, and we don't take it lightly.

    The Role

    What Founders Board Members Do

    The Founders Board role spans all three boards. It's not a higher tier — it's a different kind of commitment, shaped around people whose perspective genuinely covers all three areas.

    Brand Board

    Amplify the Mission

    Founders help shape how AESOP communicates its value to the world — to parents, educators, employers, and policymakers. Their voices and networks extend AESOP's reach beyond what any marketing budget could achieve.

    Education Board

    Protect the Curriculum

    They engage with course content at the same depth as dedicated Education Board members — questioning assumptions, flagging gaps, and ensuring that what AESOP teaches genuinely prepares learners for an AI-shaped world.

    Technical Board

    Validate the Platform

    They hold the platform to a high standard — accuracy, security, accessibility, and integrity. Founders understand that a trustworthy platform is the foundation everything else is built on.

    Shared Values

    The Conviction That Brings Them Here

    🧒

    Childhood Education as a Priority

    Founders believe that AI literacy introduced early — at the right developmental stage, in the right way — shapes how the next generation thinks, creates, and leads. This is not a corporate priority for them. It's personal.

    🌍

    Accessibility Over Exclusivity

    The best AI education should not be reserved for well-funded schools or families who can afford it. Founders are drawn to AESOP precisely because its mission is to close that gap — not exploit it.

    🔬

    Rigor Without Gatekeeping

    Making AI education accessible doesn't mean making it shallow. Founders insist on both — high-quality, accurate, pedagogically sound content that doesn't require a PhD to engage with.

    🤝

    Long-Term Commitment

    Founders are here for the arc of the mission, not the moment. They understand that transforming AI education takes time, iteration, and sustained belief — and they've signed up for that.
