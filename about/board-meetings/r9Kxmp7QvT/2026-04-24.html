AI Standards, Course Review & Auto-Improvement
35:45
Scott Schindler
We now map every course against six standards: two U.S. AI standards (ISTE and CSTA), two international (UNESCO and the EU AI Act), NIST AI RMF, and one more. I'm not including ISO 42001 because it's a paid standard — most people aren't paying for it and will use NIST instead. There's now a review page that runs our rubric against three AI models to score course quality, and also scores against all six standards. Then there's a button — I built it this week but haven't tested it yet — that picks an individual course, tells AI to review the whole course, compare against scores on each standard, and then add one or two modules where we're weakest. It'll write the modules, then rescore. The technical team ensures it works, the education team ensures the scores improve.
