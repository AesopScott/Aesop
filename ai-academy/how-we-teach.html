The Research

The Science of Youth Engagement

Education research shows three critical gaps between how youth learn best and how most courses are taught.

Relevance Matters

When students can identify content as relevant to their lives, motivation and achievement increase significantly. Abstract lessons without context feel disconnected and lose engagement fast.

Visual Learning Works

65% of students are visual learners. Yet most educational content relies on text-heavy explanations. Visual aids and diagrams produce better outcomes and faster comprehension.

Hands-On Beats Passive

Students who engage in hands-on, problem-solving activities are 1.5x less likely to fail than passive learners. Yet many courses offer no hands-on component at all.
