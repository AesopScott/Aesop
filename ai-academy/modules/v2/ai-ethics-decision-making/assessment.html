F · Gaps & Intentional Scope Boundaries
Domains Not Covered By This Course
Prompt Engineering Generative AI RAG / Knowledge Systems AI Agents & Systems AI Security Data Analytics
These gaps are intentional. This course is a domain specialist: its 8 modules concentrate all instructional time on ethics, governance, bias, documentation, explainability, and policy. Covering 9 of 28 blueprint skills at Apply/Analyze depth produces stronger outcomes than shallow coverage of all 28. Students who need the remaining 19 skills should complete the corresponding specialist courses in the AESOP catalog. The blueprint scoring reflects depth of coverage, not a deficiency.
G · Portfolio Artifact
M8 Build — Capstone Project

The Governance Proposal

A complete AI governance framework designed for a city of 280,000 people. Students identify high-risk AI systems in municipal operations, establish an oversight structure with defined roles and accountability chains, draft a policy for responsible AI procurement and deployment, specify incident response and audit procedures, and justify every design decision against current legal and ethical frameworks.

This artifact reaches Bloom's Create — the course ceiling. It is retained as a portfolio artifact demonstrating the student can synthesize governance knowledge into a novel, defensible document.
