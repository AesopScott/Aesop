Value Paragraphs

Two to three sentences. Use it in introductions, bios, grant letters, or the "about" moment in a meeting.
Version A — The Civic Case
AI literacy is the civil rights issue of this decade.

Artificial intelligence is already making consequential decisions about hiring, lending, healthcare, and criminal justice — yet the vast majority of people affected by those decisions have never received a single hour of AI education. AESOP AI Academy was built to change that. Our free, multilingual curriculum reaches learners across three tracks — Youth, Young Adult, and Professional — in 13 languages, at no cost to the people who need it most.

Access is the mission. Everything else is infrastructure.

Angle: equity and civic responsibility — best for policy, nonprofit, and philanthropic audiences
Version B — The Economic Case
The workforce AI gap is a trillion-dollar problem. AESOP is part of the solution.

McKinsey estimates that up to 375 million workers will need to reskill by 2030 due to AI-driven automation — and the education infrastructure to absorb that demand doesn't yet exist at scale. AESOP AI Academy delivers 75+ structured courses across governance, ethics, development, and applied AI, free of charge, to any learner anywhere in the world.

We operate lean, scale through automation, and grow faster than traditional curriculum development cycles ever could.

Angle: economic and workforce — best for business, corporate, and investor audiences
Version C — The Education Case
Great AI education shouldn't require a great zip code.

The best AI literacy resources today cost hundreds of dollars, require existing technical fluency, or are locked behind institutional subscriptions that most schools and learners can't afford. AESOP AI Academy exists to make none of that true. Our story-driven curriculum meets learners where they are — whether they're eight years old or fifty-eight, in a classroom in Nairobi or a boardroom in New York.

Sixty courses. Thirteen languages. One price: free.

Angle: education equity — best for K–12, higher ed, and community organization audiences

30-Second Elevator Pitches

~90 words, spoken aloud. Memorize one. Use it in hallways, on panels, at dinners. Each version closes with a different ask.
30 Seconds Version A — The Board Ask
Lead the institution that's closing the AI literacy gap.

Every organization on earth is grappling with AI — and almost no one is prepared to lead through it. AESOP AI Academy is the free, open platform that changes that. Sixty courses, thirteen languages, three learning tracks, zero cost to learners.

We don't have a paywall problem. We have a scale problem — and that's a solvable one. What we need is board-level leadership to build institutional partnerships, unlock long-term funding, and carry AI literacy to the billion people who urgently need it but will never pay for it.

The window to shape this is open now. It won't stay open long.

Closes with: an invitation to join the board
30 Seconds Version B — The Donor Ask
Fund the infrastructure that keeps AI education free.

Imagine a student in rural India, a nurse in São Paulo, and a city council member in Detroit — all getting the same quality AI education, in their own language, for free. That's what AESOP AI Academy does today.

We've built 75+ courses across 13 languages with a team and budget that would make most EdTech companies laugh. What we haven't built yet is the financial runway to guarantee that access for the next decade.

Your investment keeps the doors open — for everyone, forever.

Closes with: a philanthropic giving ask
30 Seconds Version C — The Partnership Ask
Bring AI literacy inside your organization — at no cost.

Your employees, students, or community members are already encountering AI every day. Most of them have no framework for understanding it, governing it, or using it well. AESOP AI Academy gives them one — free.

Sixty structured courses, professional and youth tracks, thirteen languages. No licensing fees. No per-seat costs. No implementation burden on your team.

All we ask in return is your co-promotion — help us reach the people who need this most, and we'll handle everything else.

Closes with: an institutional partnership ask
