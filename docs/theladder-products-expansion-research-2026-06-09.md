# The Ladder Products Expansion Research

Date researched: 2026-06-09

Purpose: candidate expansion set for `/theladder-products/`. This adds ten missing product categories, each with 25 products that are not already listed in `docs/theladder-products-catalog.md`.

## Research Notes

The current Products Ladder already covers broad assistants, productivity suites, coding tools, RAG/search infrastructure, data/BI platforms, creative generation, audio/video tools, GTM/customer support, automation/agents, model APIs, and a small regulated vertical block.

These ten categories fill gaps where learners are likely to encounter AI inside real operational software:

- HR, recruiting, and talent intelligence
- Education, tutoring, and classroom tools
- Ecommerce, retail, and merchandising AI
- Finance, accounting, FP&A, procurement, and tax
- AIOps, observability, and incident response
- AI governance, model risk, privacy, and compliance
- Architecture, construction, and real estate
- Manufacturing, supply chain, logistics, and industrial AI
- Scientific, biotech, lab, and clinical AI
- Personal productivity, notes, email, calendar, and knowledge work

## Implementation Notes

- Treat this as an expansion queue, not an automatic replacement for the current top 250.
- Add a `categoryGroup` or `expansionTrack` field before merging into the Products page so learners can filter the current core catalog separately from specialized product tracks.
- A sensible first implementation would add three expansion tabs: `Workforce`, `Operations`, and `Personal`.
- Course depth should be conservative:
  - `B/I/A`: governance, AIOps, finance controls, supply chain, clinical/scientific, and construction tools with significant professional risk.
  - `B/I`: most HR, education, ecommerce, and productivity tools.
  - `B`: narrow utilities such as simple note capture, review generation, or single-purpose classroom helpers.
