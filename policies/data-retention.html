Data Retention & Disposal Policy

What data we collect, how long we keep it, how we archive it, and how it is securely and permanently deleted.

Version: 1.0.0 Effective: April 23, 2026 Reviewed by: Scott Schindler, CISSP

What We Collect

AESOP AI Academy collects only the minimum data necessary to deliver educational services. The following categories describe what we may hold:

Data TypeExamplesCollected From
Account / IdentityName, email address, organization nameRegistration form
Educator Learning DataCourse progress, module completion, quiz scores — for registered educator-users taking our courses (not K–12 student records)Platform activity
Usage AnalyticsPage views, session duration, feature interactionsFirebase Analytics (anonymized)

We Do Not Collect Social Security numbers, government IDs, financial account numbers, health records, biometric data, or any sensitive personal information not required to deliver educational services.

Archiving

AESOP AI Academy does not maintain long-term archives of personally identifiable information. We do not export PII to cold storage, third-party archiving services, or backup tapes that persist beyond the retention windows above.

Aggregated, anonymized learning analytics may be retained indefinitely for platform improvement purposes. No individual can be identified from these aggregates.

Disposal Method

When data reaches its retention limit or a deletion request is fulfilled:

  • Data is securely deleted from all production databases and backup systems
  • Deletion is permanent and irreversible — we do not maintain deleted data in recoverable form
  • Data is never sold, licensed, or transferred to third parties for any commercial purpose
  • Third-party service providers are contractually required to delete data on our instruction
No Data Brokering AESOP AI Academy does not participate in data brokering, behavioral advertising networks, or any commercial arrangement that involves sharing learner data with third parties for non-educational purposes.

Institutional Deployments (Districts & Organizations)

AESOP AI Academy does not collect K–12 student data. When a school district, government agency, or other institution deploys our platform for educator professional development:

  • No K–12 student education records are transmitted to, collected by, or stored in our platform
  • The educator-users enrolled by the institution are the learners; their account and progress data is not a student education record under FERPA
  • Upon contract termination, all educator-user account data is deleted within 30 days
  • A Service Agreement is available for all institutional partners upon request
Service Agreement for Institutional Deployments We provide a Service Agreement covering educator-user data, usage terms, and accountability commitments. Contact: partnerships@aesopacademy.org

Individual Deletion Requests

Individual users may request deletion of all personal data associated with their account at any time. Requests are fulfilled within 30 days.

To submit a deletion request: privacy@aesopacademy.org

For users under 13, a parent or guardian must submit the request. Identity verification may be required.

Policy Updates

This policy is reviewed annually or when material changes occur to our data practices. Institutional partners will be notified of material changes with at least 30 days' notice.
