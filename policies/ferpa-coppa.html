Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act

When contracted by an educational institution — school, school district, university, or government agency — AESOP AI Academy operates as a "school official" under FERPA. This means we have a legitimate educational interest in accessing student education records solely to deliver the contracted services, and we are bound by the same FERPA obligations as the institution itself.

Our FERPA Commitments

  • Student education records remain the exclusive property of the institution at all times
  • We do not disclose education records to any third party without institutional authorization, except as required by law
  • Student data is used only to provide and improve the contracted educational service — never for advertising, profiling, or commercial purposes
  • We will execute a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with every institutional partner before accessing any student data
  • All student data is deleted upon contract termination per the schedule agreed in the DPA
  • Institutions retain the right to inspect, correct, and delete student education records held by Aesop at any time

Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

A standard DPA is available for immediate execution. We support custom terms negotiated by the district's legal team at no additional cost. To request a DPA:

Request a DPA Email partnerships@aesopacademy.org with your institution name, district size, and anticipated deployment scope. We will respond within 3 business days.
COPPA 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501–6506 · 16 C.F.R. Part 312

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

For learners under 13 years of age, AESOP AI Academy applies heightened protections required by COPPA. Full details are in our Privacy Notice. In summary:

  • Verifiable parental or guardian consent is required before account creation for users under 13
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary to deliver the learning experience
  • No behavioral advertising to users under 13, ever
  • No data sale or sharing with third parties for commercial purposes
  • Data for users under 13 is deleted within 30 days of a verified deletion request or account closure
  • No persistent tracking across third-party sites

For institutional deployments serving under-13 learners, the school or district provides consent on behalf of parents in accordance with the COPPA school exception (16 C.F.R. § 312.5(b)(1)).
