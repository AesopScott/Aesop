Student Privacy Compliance

AESOP AI Academy does not collect K–12 student data. Our platform serves educators and professionals — here is how student privacy is protected by design, and what that means for institutional procurement.

Version: 1.0.0 Effective: April 23, 2026 Service Agreement: Available on request
FERPA 20 U.S.C. § 1232g · 34 C.F.R. Part 99

Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act

No Student Education Records AESOP AI Academy does not collect, hold, or process K–12 student education records. FERPA's data-handler obligations — school-official status, student-data DPAs — are not triggered by our platform.

Our platform is designed for educators, administrators, and professionals — not K–12 students. When a district deploys Aesop for educator professional development, no student data flows to us. FERPA governs how vendors handle student education records; because we hold none, it does not apply to our platform as a data-handler obligation.

Privacy by Design

  • No student roster data is required to deploy or use AESOP AI Academy
  • No K–12 student accounts, grade records, or education records are created or stored in our systems
  • Educator-users authenticate with their own credentials — no student data is involved at any stage
  • The AESOP Engine (our curriculum AI) receives no student data as inputs at any stage

For Procurement Teams

Districts often ask whether a student-data DPA is required. Because we hold no student education records, a student-data DPA is not applicable. We provide a Service Agreement for institutional deployments covering educator-user accounts, usage terms, and accountability commitments.

Request a Service Agreement Email partnerships@aesopacademy.org with your institution name and deployment scope. We respond within 3 business days.
COPPA 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501–6506 · 16 C.F.R. Part 312

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

AESOP AI Academy is designed for adult learners — educators, administrators, and professionals. Our platform is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. Full details are in our Privacy Notice.

  • Our platform is designed for adult educators — not K–12 students
  • We do not collect data from users under 13
  • No behavioral advertising to any user, ever
  • No data sale or sharing with third parties for commercial purposes
  • No persistent tracking across third-party sites
